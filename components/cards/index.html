Component Overview

To initialize a card, apply the .card class to a containing <div>. A card is made up of three sections, a header, a body, and a footer. The card header will get the .card__header class. The card body will get the .card__body class. The card footer will get the .card__footer class.

The card body can consume either a Data Table or a Tile.
